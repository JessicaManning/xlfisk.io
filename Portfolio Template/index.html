About

Hello! my name is Jessica Manning and I'm a freelance artist from the southern Connecticut. I'm currently pursing my BFA around the Boston area while majoring in Animation and Interactive Media.

I'm interested in Pre-Production work such as Storyboarding and Character Design but also have a passion for Animation and Illustration. My work so far is alot of experimental work from college with a few spots of personal work I've done in my free time.

Experience

Fisk Website Developer

For a semester, myself along with fellow classmates, were to design a full fuctioning wedsite for Fisk, a world renowned pipe organ company. We designed dozen of paper prototypes for the navigation and layout of the website. We also created a basic mobile wesite for the company that involed showcasing one of their case study.

MASH-UP Film Festival Producer

For the past two years now my co-producer and I have been running the annul MASH-UP Film Festival at the Cabot Cinema in Beverly, MA. We've been in charge of ogranizing, fundraising, pubilcizing, and hosting this event to promote local artist that focus around ditial and time-based media. This showcase was created by alumi of Montserrat College of Art and we kept it going for newer generations of artists as well as opened up for more types of media so more people can feel welcome and have a proper setting to display their artwork.
